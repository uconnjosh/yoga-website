SUNSET CLIFFS PILATES

Shaping, sculpting, and strengthening bodies, inside and out!

You will find our beautiful pilates studio conveniently located 1 block from the ocean. We offer private and semi-private sessions, with daily Spin and Pilates Mat Classes, including classes in Reformer, Cadillac/Trapeze, Wunda Chair and Spine Corrector Equipment. Our friendly fitness studio staff of experienced and certified instructors will assist you in achieving your personal health and fitness goals. Sunset Cliffs Pilates can help you transform your body into the best shape of your life! Pilates articulates the joints, it dictates coordination, and it is an isometric workout using the resistance of your body weight, employing several muscle groups simultaneously through the concept spring resistance. Hundred of movements can be performed on all the equipment, strengthening the core of the body as well as other desired areas.
